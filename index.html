What We Do

Everything your front desk does, automated

One AI receptionist. Six core capabilities. Zero missed calls.

AI Call Answering

Picks up every inbound call in under 2 seconds, 24/7, greeting callers with your business name and handling the conversation end-to-end.

Appointment Booking

Checks your live calendar and books appointments directly during the call. No double-booking, no back-and-forth, no delays.

SMS Follow-up

Sends instant confirmation texts after every call. Customers have their booking details before they even hang up.

Lead Capture

Collects caller name, number, and intent on every call and logs it to your dashboard automatically. No lead slips through.

Live Dashboard

Outbound Callbacks

Web leads get an automatic callback within minutes of submitting an inquiry. If they don't pick up, an SMS follow-up goes out automatically.
